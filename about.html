About Us

Our mandate is to make books available to everyone no matter the age, gender or tribal affiliation. "Africans don't read" is a stereotype we are not comfortable with and we have a mandate to challenge that. We believe exposure to the right reading materials will go a long way in proferring solutions to many of the continent's unique problems and set us on par with much developed worlds. Readers need access to books that engage their imagination and spark their interest so that they are inspired to start reading and keep reading through their lives.At Better Reads, we provide access to a global collection of high-quality digital books to engage readers around the world. You’re in the right place. Tell us what titles or genres you’ve enjoyed in the past, and we’ll give you surprisingly insightful recommendations.
